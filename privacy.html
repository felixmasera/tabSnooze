TabSnooze — Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 2026

TabSnooze is a browser extension that lets you snooze tabs for later. This policy explains how your data is handled.

Data we collect

TabSnooze does not collect, transmit, or share any personal data. All information stays on your device.

What is stored locally

  • URLs and titles of snoozed tabs
  • Snooze schedules and labels you create
  • Your extension settings (reminder times, language preference)

This data is stored using the browser's built-in storage API (chrome.storage) and never leaves your browser.

Third-party services

TabSnooze uses Google's favicon service (google.com/s2/favicons) to display website icons. No personal data is sent — only the domain of the website is used.

Permissions

  • tabs — to read the URL and title of the current tab when you snooze it
  • storage — to save your snoozed tabs and settings locally
  • alarms — to schedule when a snoozed tab should reappear
  • notifications — to alert you when a snoozed tab is ready
  • contextMenus — to let you snooze links via right-click
